Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026  ·  Pratt & Associates / Rental Analyst

This Privacy Policy explains how Rental Analyst ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Pratt & Associates, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our website at rental-analyst.com.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create a free account:

  • First name — to personalize your experience
  • Email address — to send occasional product updates
  • Password — stored securely, never shared

We also collect anonymous usage data through Google Analytics (page views, session duration, general location by region) to help us improve the product. No personally identifiable information is sent to Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To send occasional product updates, new features, and tips (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve the Rental Analyst product based on usage patterns
  • We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

3. Property Data

Property analyses you run are stored locally in your browser (localStorage) and are not transmitted to our servers. If you save properties to your portfolio, that data remains on your device unless you clear your browser storage.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RentCast API — to fetch rent estimates and property data. See RentCast's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics — for anonymous usage analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Sheets — email addresses submitted at signup are stored in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Pratt & Associates.

5. Cookies

We use browser localStorage (not cookies) to store your account session and saved properties. Google Analytics uses cookies to track anonymous usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

6. Data Retention

We retain your email address and name for as long as you wish to receive updates from us. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us at the address below.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from emails at any time

8. Children's Privacy

Rental Analyst is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of any material changes via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.
